500 Guns is a full time family run gunshop. We have been in business for over 30 years and are still going strong. We carry primarily used handguns and long guns. We also carry fine English and European double guns and we are a Merkel dealer. At times we have U.S. and foreign military firearms well as edge weapons. If you are looking for something we don't have listed, give us a call as our inventory changes daily. We are located 3 blocks east of the Indianapolis Speedway on West 16th street. If you are in town for the Indianapolis 500 or one of the others races please stop by.
